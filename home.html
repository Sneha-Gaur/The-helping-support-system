How It Works

Our platform is simple and easy to use. Here's how it works:

  1. Report an Issue: Submit your problems related to water, healthcare, education, roads, and more.
  2. Track Solutions: Stay updated on the progress of your reported issues.
  3. Government Intervention: The relevant authorities are notified to take action and resolve the problem.
